Unlike standard sentiment analysis pipelines that treat emojis as noise, this engine implements a custom Feature Engineering layer. During EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis), we observed that 49% of the training corpus contained emojis, which showed a stronger correlation with sentiment labels than text length.

To capitalize on this, the pipeline amplifies extracted symbols with a weight coefficient of \( w=5 \) before vectorization. The core classification is handled by Multinomial Naive Bayes, which was selected after benchmarking against Logistic Regression and Linear SVM for its superior performance on sparse text data.
