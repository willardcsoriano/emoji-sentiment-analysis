{# ── EXAMPLE 2: CLEAR NEGATIVE — SARCASM VETO ── #}
02 "i love having bugs 😭" Clear Negative Sarcasm · Veto Fires

Feature Extraction

emoji_pos_count 0 —
emoji_neg_count 10 😭 × BOOST 10
word_pos_count 1 "love"
word_neg_count 0 —

Score Computation (pre-veto)

word_pos × +0.856 +0.856
emoji_neg × −0.049 −0.490
bias + remaining tokens ≈ +0.5
logit ≈ +0.87
sigmoid(0.87) ≈ Positive ✗

emoji coef (−0.049) too weak to override "love" — model is fooled

⚡ Veto Triggered

emoji_neg_count = 10 (> 0)

model prediction = Positive → conflict detected

word_pos_count = 1 (insufficient to suppress)

→ override to Negative · confidence rescaled to 0.9318

Output
NEGATIVE · 93.18% confidence · Veto Applied
