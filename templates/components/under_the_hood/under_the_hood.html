Under the Hood

The model is not a black box. Every prediction is the result of measurable arithmetic — token weights, polarity counts, a dot product, and a deterministic veto check. Ten worked examples, from raw text to numerical output.

Each row shows the exact feature extraction, the logistic regression score breakdown, the veto decision, and the final output. Expand any example to see the full calculation. The sarcasm case is open by default — it tells the best story.
