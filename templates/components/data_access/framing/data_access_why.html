Engineering Insight: Symbolic Signals as Structured Data

EDA confirmed that 49.3% of the corpus contains at least one emoji — common enough to engineer around, sparse enough to treat as a binary anchor rather than a frequency signal. Rather than letting emojis dissolve passively into TF-IDF tokenisation, the pipeline extracts them as four structured polarity counts via manually curated lexicons. These signals operate orthogonally to word-based statistics and delivered a measured +0.044 F1 improvement over the text-only baseline.

Design Principle: Feature Augmentation Over Token Absorption Signal Class: Auxiliary / Symbolic
