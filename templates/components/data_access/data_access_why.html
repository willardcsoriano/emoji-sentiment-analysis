Engineering Insight: Symbolic Signals as Structured Data

Exploratory analysis revealed that symbolic expressions are not peripheral artifacts but central carriers of affect in short-form discourse. Rather than allowing emojis to be absorbed passively into tokenization, the system references a curated polarity lexicon to extract structured, non-linguistic sentiment features. These signals act orthogonally to word-based statistics, enriching the representation space and materially improving classification robustness.

Design Principle: Feature Augmentation Over Token Absorption Signal Class: Auxiliary / Symbolic
