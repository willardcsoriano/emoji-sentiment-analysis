Why Emoji Sentiment Referencing Matters

Emoji sentiment is frequently embedded within tweet text but can be diluted or lost during conventional text vectorization. This engineering step preserves symbolic affect (emotional expression) by detecting emojis and referencing a polarity lexicon to derive auxiliary sentiment signals. By augmenting textual features with emoji-informed context, the pipeline retains emotional nuance while maintaining model interpretability and stability.
