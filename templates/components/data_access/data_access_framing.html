Data Scale & Engineering Constraints

Note on Scale: This pipeline operates in a low-resource environment, utilizing a compact training corpus of 1,000 labeled tweets. While modest in scale, this constraint necessitates a focus on feature density and model interpretability—making linear classification an optimal choice to prevent overfitting while maintaining high predictive transparency.

The core engineering objective is the fusion of dual-channel sentiment signals: linguistic text and symbolic emojis. By reconciling these distinct modalities into a unified feature space, the system captures emotional nuance that is frequently lost in standard tokenization pipelines—proving that even at a 1k-sample scale, targeted feature engineering can drive significant performance gains.
