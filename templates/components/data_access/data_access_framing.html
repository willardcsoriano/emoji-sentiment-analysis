Data Access & Engineering Constraints

The dataset is intentionally limited to approximately 1,000 labeled samples. This constraint prioritizes interpretability and rapid iteration over raw performance, making classical linear models and feature-weighted approaches more reliable than high-capacity neural architectures in this context.

Sentiment is not expressed through language alone. This system ingests two structurally incompatible datasets to capture both linguistic sentiment and symbolic emotional signals. The primary engineering challenge lies in unifying these sources without introducing bias or information loss.
