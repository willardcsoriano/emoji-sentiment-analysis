Linguistic and symbolic signals are transformed into structured model inputs. Text is vectorized using TF-IDF weighting, while emoji-derived polarity signals are encoded as auxiliary sentiment features.

Text Features

  • • Tokenization
  • • N-gram extraction
  • • TF-IDF weighting

Emoji Features

  • • Polarity indicators
  • • Presence signals
  • • Aggregated sentiment scores

Feature spaces are concatenated to form the final model training matrix.
