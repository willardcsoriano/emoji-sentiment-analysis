Vectorization Strategy

$$ \mathrm{tf\mbox{-}idf}_{t,d} = \mathrm{tf}_{t,d}\cdot \log\frac{N}{\mathrm{df}_t} $$

TF-IDF emphasizes high-entropy emotional markers while down-weighting frequent grammatical structures that contribute limited sentiment signal.

Classification Model

$$ P(c \mid x) \propto P(c) \prod_{i=1}^{n} P(x_i \mid c) $$

Multinomial Naive Bayes estimates class membership under conditional independence, favoring interpretability and stable probabilistic outputs.
